Be the best version of yourself !


Are you wanting healthier and more balanced relationships in your life?



I offer individual guidance sessions to help you develop positive relationships in your life. I focus on assisting you to develop awareness of yourself. By cultivating your relationship to spirituality you can see the whole world through a meaningful perspective. The more you learn to love yourself and your own divinity, the easier it becomes to relate to others.

Are you wondering how to heal from traumatic experiences?



I facilitate workshops for Conscious Connection. We integrate intimacy games with partners to explore the experience of seeing and being seen. We practice mindful yoga to learn about the internal experience of living in a body. We share our truths and learn from one another. We witness and hold space for one another. We connect from our hearts and understand the way we all reflect each other.
