Personal Coaching

I offer individual guidance sessions to help you develop positive relationships in your life. I focus on assisting you to develop awareness of yourself. By cultivating your relationship to spirituality you can see the whole world through a meaningful perspective. The more you learn to love yourself and your own divinity, the easier it becomes to relate to others.



Topics which Lolly be would cover

Healing from Abusive Relationships

Emotional, Physical and Sexual Abuse in any intimate personal relationship is a trauma that requires strength to heal. This strength can blossom into an endless supply of divine love energy. One has to heal the body, from the physical manifestations of dis-ease and imbalance in the body. One must heal the mind, by developing mindfulness, we can change our thoughts, our feelings are influenced, and our actions follow. The neuroplasticity of the brain is phenomenal, we can actually rewire our brains.

Sacred Sexuality

For a human, one of our most divine and intense experiences is that of sexuality. This aspect has the potential to communicate and embody messages which cannot be put into words. Physical sexuality is a profound and vulnerable experience. When paired with love, it can be truly sacred. Many of us are not taught how to appreciate and healthily express this aspect of ourselves. Developing intimacy with one another is about opening up on several dimensions of connection with another being.

Adoption

People who were adopted, and people who are adopting, anyone interested in adoption! I was adopted from another country and grew up in the USA. I have facilitated several girls workshops for adopted girls utilizing group work for self expression. With Lutheran Social Services.

Healthy Relationships

Lolly be is an expert in managing domestic violence or intimate partner violence. She help people develop a healthy relationship. She went through it before as a result of that she began to study relationships.

Tarot Card Readings

I trained in Rishikesh, India in 2014. I utilize the powers of the kabbalah through the Tarot cards and provide an interpretation of the energies showing up around you right now. I do not predict the future, I provide guidance on the current themes as they stand today. This allows you a fresh perspective on the dynamics present for you at this moment. We can then draw upon this to facilitate a guidance dialogue.



Contact lolly to arrange a workshop for her to do or a lecture or presentation to offer on any of these topics.
